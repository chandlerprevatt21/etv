Business Details

Business Name
Computer Wish
Description
Computer Wish was started from the frustration of an average person, living an average day-to-day life in an above average world. The frustration then blossomed into an idea from the average person not being able to contact large companies or corporations when there was something very important that needed to be communicated directly. No message should wait days on a voicemail.
​
Our mission is to use traditional values when answering calls for our customers. We believe in professionalism, quality, timeliness and most of all excellent customer service, with you as the customers and to all your customers.
​
At Computer Wish, we love new ideas and technology, hence the name, but we also love people. So, we started with average, hardworking people that care about their companies and reputations, just like you and developed into us helping you build and make dreams happen.
