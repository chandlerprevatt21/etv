Village@Work™: This workforce development program is focused on creating and funding jobs, internships, and apprenticeships within Black-owned businesses that need additional personnel to enhance their success. It creates a win-win for our business owners and job/career seekers.
Village Strivers™: This ETV program will provide academic enrichment, homework help, coaching and tutoring, college and test prep resources for youth and job training for adults and youth (virtually or in-person).
The program will be implemented in partnership with Village volunteers and paid teachers of color who serve as role models and mentors. ETV will fund individual and group sessions with enrichment providers and independent tutors, emphasizing math, science and writing skills.
