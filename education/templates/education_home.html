EDUCATION

ETV’s Education initiatives are designed to prepare The Village for success. Our programs empower students and other community members to leverage academic enrichment and vocational training, and prepare for college success.

State Of The Village: Did You Know...?

~23% of the Black adult population has a bachelor’s degree or higher (versus ~36% of White adults)

A college degree, whether an Associate or Bachelor’s degree, is more and more vital. The number of “good-paying jobs” that did not require some level of college education or technical training have continued to rapidly decrease.
