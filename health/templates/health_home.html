PEACE

ETV's Peace pillar has a regional footprint focused on improving the mental and physical health of the Black community. It addresses the lack of affordable access to quality healthcare and other health-related concerns plaguing the Black community.

In addition, ETV champions and executes programs that foster cultural pride, self-confidence and self-respect, promote a growth mindset and provide critical information.

The number of reported cases of diagnosed mental illness, especially anxiety and depression, as well as the rate of suicide in the Black community, continues to climb in recent years. The racial disparity between Black Americans and other races with respect to physical health challenges also continues to rise.

Health & Wellness Initiatives
Knowledge is Power!™
Fitness, physical and mental health programs, including COVID-19 education, testing and vaccination support, are executed in partnership with The Village - churches, community organizations and healthcare providers.
  • ETV is developing this platform to give the Black community free and affordable access to mental health professionals, especially Black psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers and other wellness professionals to improve emotional health and wellness in the Black youth and adult communities. This "VAP" program will mirror the offerings of large corporations through their "EAP" - Employee Assistance Programs - but will primarily enlist practitioners of color with the cultural competence and personal experience to fully support and connect with their patients.
